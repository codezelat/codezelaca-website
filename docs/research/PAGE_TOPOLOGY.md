# CCA Homepage Topology

Source: `https://cca.it.com/` captured at 1440×1000, 768×900, and 390×844.

1. Fixed pill header — static navigation at desktop; menu-trigger overlay at mobile.
2. Hero — pink band, cohort notice, main offer, two CTAs, proof row, source hero artwork, four metrics.
3. Programme overview — copy/collage at left and four outcome cards at right; cards stack on mobile and collage hides.
4. Differentiators — centered heading, three cards on each side of the CCA mark; six-card stack on mobile.
5. Programs — autoplay/drag carousel, 3/2/1 visible cards, pagination, all-programs CTA.
6. Recognition — autoplay/drag logo carousel, 5/3/2 visible marks, pagination.
7. Consultation CTA — framed source artwork background, centered brand lockup, copy, two CTAs.
8. Footer — dark rounded three-column surface and legal row; centered vertical flow on mobile.
