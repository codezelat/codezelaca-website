# Refund Policy Specification

## Route and intent

- Route: `/refund-policy/`
- Effective copy date: July 22, 2026.
- Purpose: explain CCA's limited refund rule clearly and consistently without changing the cloned 2025 Terms and Conditions.

## Core rule

- Fees are non-refundable except when CCA permanently cancels the enrolled programme because CCA cannot continue delivery, or where applicable law requires a remedy.
- Rescheduling, temporary suspension, trainer or venue changes, curriculum changes and comparable delivery alternatives are not treated as cancellation.
- Before delivery begins, eligible refunds cover programme fees actually received; after delivery begins, eligible refunds are limited to the undelivered proportion.
- The policy expressly preserves rights and remedies that cannot lawfully be excluded under Sri Lankan law.

This is newly drafted operational/legal copy rather than cloned live-site wording. It requires qualified Sri Lankan legal review before production publication.
